Short Bio

I am currently an assistant professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications. I graduated in 2023 from the National Key Laboratory for Virtual Reality Technology and Systems, School of Computer Science, Beihang University, supervised by Prof. Lili Wang. In 2024, I joined Prof. Qingshan Liu's team at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications. My research primarily focuses on virtual/mixed reality, real-time rendering, human-computer interaction, and computer graphics. I have published over 20 academic papers in top international journals and conferences in the field, including IEEE TVCG, IEEE VR, IEEE ISMAR, and IEEE TCSVT, with 8 papers as the first author or corresponding author, and I hold 5 granted patents. I am leading projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) Young Scientists Fund (C class), the Natural Science Foundation of the Jiangsu Higher Education Institutions of China, and Open Project Program of State Key Laboratory of Virtual Reality Technology and Systems. I have participated in national key research and development programs, key projects of the NSFC, and Beijing municipal science and technology programs. I serve as an executive committee member of the China Computer Federation Virtual Reality and Visualization Technology (CCF-TCVRV).
